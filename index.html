Delhizine
A zine is a publication made out of a single sheet of paper folded up. Delhizine is a collective I started with a friend, seeking to create a community of zine-makers and readers. All hand-to-hand distribution word-of-mouth advertising, because physical media may survive us all in all the ways it matters.
Research
A few projects in astronomy and computational physics such as Implementing numerical schemes for solving problems in MHD, volunteering at projects at IUCAA, outreach at Sky Watchers' Association of North Bengal, as well as DIY astrophotography and processing.

A few projects in Quantum Computing on my own - MBQC, TQC, etc. presented at conferences and quite a few paper-reading competitions.
